About Me

I'm Yuvraj

Software Engineer | Full Stack Developer | Android Developer

It all began when I was just in the 6th standard, and the world of coding opened up to me through HTML. I was immediately drawn to the world of web development, and with the aid of resources like w3schools, I quickly mastered a range of languages including PHP, MySQL, HTML5, JavaScript, CSS, Bootstrap, and jQuery.

My thirst for knowledge led me to explore the realm of app development, where I embraced the challenge of mastering Android development and Java, utilizing the rich educational resources of Udemy and Udacity. In tandem, my venture into the world of blogging, although not an instant success, provided me with invaluable insights into SEO, digital marketing, and advertising.

My journey truly took flight with the launch of my first real-world project, Be Conteur, which gained significant traction after being featured on platforms such as Product Hunt and BetaList. Encouraged by this success, I went on to create several other successful application.

After I got enrolled into college, I joined most of the technical clubs and eventually became Join Secratory of ExtremeCSE, PR and Outreach Head of Loop Club and App Development Head of Google Developer Students Club, GCET.

In First year itself, I got internship in Full Stack Development at Oopar Club, a Bangalore based funded startup. Also, I won Pitch Tank organized by Galgotias Extreme Club.

In my second year of college, my team won second prize in ACM Hackathon at GGSIPU(Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University), New Delhi.

In my third year of college, I led my team to winning Smart India Hackathon - 2023, getting featured in news articles.
